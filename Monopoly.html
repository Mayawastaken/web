Monopoly

When one thinks of a board game, most people immediately think of one: Monopoly! Worthfully so, it is one of the most popular board games in the US! Players take turns rolling dice, moving their character around the board, trying to get monopolies. This game requires skill and a touch of luck (well ok, actually a lot of luck :)). It is super fun to play against your friends and family, and it is definitely worth it to bring to game night. To find out more about the game Monopoly, click here!
