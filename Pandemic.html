Pandemic

There are many amazingly fun board games out there that aren't as known to most, and this is one of them. This, unlike many board games, is a cooperative board game where players try to stop a pandemic from spreading and save the world (if only we could do that to COVID...)! Each player has a special strength, and players try to come up with strategies to enhance those strengths, find cures, take them to cities, and save the world.
